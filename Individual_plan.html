The Individual Plan

Our Individual Plan is designed to help you stand out on the pitch. This plan focuses on the three fundamentals that every elite player must dominate: Shooting, Passing, and 1v1 Play.

See the Breakdown

Mastering the Perfect Shot

Just you, the ball, and good technique are required; no keeper, or even cones are needed. We go over the fundamentals that every player should know in this video:

  • Chest up, body over the ball – Helps keep your shot down and balanced
  • Take a deep breath before shooting. Stay calm stay in control.
  • The run up is important; take a few short, angled steps with zero rush.
  • Use your laces to strike the ball by locking your ankle, pointing your toe downward, and hit through the ball.
  • Keep an eye on the bottom of your other foot. Position it next to the ball as you run up, pointing it in the direction of the goal.
Ballers tip:

Do this before or after your training. You don’t need a keeper — just focus on your body doing the same movement every time.

Accurate Passing

Good passing is about simplicity and precision. Here's how to master the short passes that keep the game moving:

  • Plant Foot: Place your non-kicking foot beside the ball, pointing towards your target.
  • Clean Contact: Use the inside of your foot for maximum control. Keep your ankle locked through the entire motion.
  • Follow Through: Your kicking foot should follow through directly at your target to ensure accuracy.
Baller's Tip:

Always be on your feet, ready to move, as shown in the video. Never pass flat-footed.

Dominating 1-on-1s

Beating a defender is about deception. This side-to-side move helps you trick opponents and get away from dangerous plays:

  • The Setup: Approach the defender under control with the ball close to your feet. Don't rush.
  • The Feint: Use a quick side-step or body feint, like the classic Ronaldo move, to make the defender commit their weight one way.
  • The Escape: As they shift, push the ball into the open space with the outside of your foot and accelerate away.
Baller's Tip:

The key is to sell the fake. Make the defender truly believe you're going one way before you explode into the other.
