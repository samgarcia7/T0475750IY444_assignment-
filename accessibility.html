About Us

Accessibility for All Ballers

On the pitch, everyone deserves a fair chance to play. We believe the same is true online.

Just as a great team communicates clearly on the pitch, a great website should communicate clearly with all its visitors. That's why this page, and our entire site, is designed with a straightforward format. A simple, clean layout ensures that everyone, regardless of ability or the technology they use, can engage with our content, understand our mission, and gain something from their visit.

Our Commitment

Our mission is to help every player decode the game, and that promise of inclusion extends to our website. We believe that everyone deserves access, so we build with a commitment to digital accessibility for people with all abilities. It's a continuous effort to improve the experience for all our users.

How We Make This Site Accessible

To make sure our website is a welcoming place for all, we focus on the following key areas:

  • Semantic HTML: The site is built with a logical code structure, so assistive technologies like screen readers can understand the content and layout easily.
  • Keyboard Navigation: All interactive elements can be accessed and operated using a keyboard alone, without needing a mouse.
  • Readable Text: We choose clear fonts and maintain a high color contrast between text and backgrounds to make reading easier for people with visual impairments.
  • Image Descriptions: All meaningful images include descriptive alternative text (alt-text) so their content is understood even if they cannot be seen.
