In-Game Intelligence

Physical skill gets you on the pitch; football intelligence keeps you there. This program is dedicated to sharpening your mind and help you read the beautiful game.

The Sequence of a High-IQ Player

We teach the three linked skills that define modern footballing genius.

Vision,Space & Movement

The best players know their next step before touching the ball. VISUALISE THEN EXECUTE

  • Scanning: scan a 360, your surroundings, check over your shoulders and find your space.
  • Intelligence: Your movements should create space for yourself and your teammates, pulling defenders out of position.
  • understand the play: Understand how usual plays breakdown. ALWAYS one step ahead of your opponent.
Baller's Tip:

first touch and next pass come before the ball does.

Decision-Making Under Pressure

The game moves fast. Choose the right option in the split-second that matters most:

  • Risk vs. Reward: Know when to attempt the 'killer pass' versus when to keep possession and recycle the play.
  • Playing Out of Trouble: Stay calm when pressed by defenders and always protect the ball while hinting to get out the situation.
  • Simplicity: Sometimes simple is better. play your pass return to keeper if needed.
Baller's Tip:

Step out your comfort zone and normalise these crucial moments. .

The First Touch

Your first touch isn't just about control- it's a weapon that sets up your next move or you lose:

  • Directional Control: Take your first touch into away from pressure, into space. This puts yourself in ahead of opponents.
  • Setting Up the next step: Use a soft touch to create the angle for your next action.
  • The get away Touch: Use a touch with more power if there is danger around,to knock the ball past a defender or force an space.
Baller's Tip:

always try to keep the ball on the floor. Even if its played in the air
